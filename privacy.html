Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

The short version: FitPulseTracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All workout data is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

Data Collection

FitPulseTracker does not collect any personal data. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any identifying information to use the app.

Data Storage

All workout data — including your exercises, sets, reps, weights, and history — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. There are no analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools embedded in the app.

Children's Privacy

We do not collect data from children or anyone. Since no personal information is collected from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is ever updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
