# FitPulseTracker Website

Landing page for [FitPulseTracker](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fitpulsetracker/id6761276830), a free iOS workout app for timed exercises, rep tracking, and weight logging.

**Live site:** [fitpulsetracker.app](https://fitpulsetracker.app)

## About the App

FitPulseTracker is a free workout app for iPhone and iPad. No subscription, no ads, no account required. Build custom workouts with timed holds, rep sets, and rest periods. Track your sets, reps, and weight automatically. Full workout history saved after every session.

Available on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/fitpulsetracker/id6761276830) for iOS 17+.

## Site Structure

The site is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) with a custom domain. Pushing to `main` triggers an automatic deploy.
